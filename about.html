About Deviantech

Who we are

Deviantech is a web consultancy with deep expertise in a broad range of internet technologies. Our primary focus is on iterative Ruby on Rails development, and we specialize in helping start-ups negotiate the tricky path from proof-of-concept to successful launch (as well as managing their subsequent growth).

What we do

We help our clients prepare, polish, and scale their applications, we contribute a variety of projects to the open source community, and we occasionally partner with other development groups on truly exciting ventures. For more information, check out our services page.
